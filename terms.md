# Terms of Service

Last updated: 2026-05-01

## Service

fsociety vpn is a personal use VPN. You get an encrypted tunnel from your device to one of our VPS endpoints, and your traffic exits to the public internet from there. Use it for lawful personal activity: privacy, geo flexibility, bypassing region locks, normal browsing.

## What you must not do

The following uses get your account terminated immediately and the key hash blocklisted, with no refund:

- Anything involving child sexual abuse material.
- Targeted attacks against specific people or organisations (port scans, credential stuffing, exploit delivery, DDoS, harassment campaigns).
- Large scale spam (email blast, comment spam, automated registrations).
- Doxxing or any activity intended to create an identifiable victim.
- Anything that produces a clear, real world victim that we can be made aware of.

We do not proactively monitor traffic. We act on credible abuse reports.

## Service interruption

This is a best effort service. There is no SLA. Servers may go offline for maintenance, provider issues, or attacks. We aim to keep at least one server in each advertised region online at all times, but we do not guarantee it.

## Account

- Your 16 digit key is the only credential. We do not store the plaintext, only a SHA256 hash.
- Lose the key, lose the account. There is no recovery flow, by design.
- Maximum 3 devices per key. Adding a 4th requires removing one of the existing devices first.
